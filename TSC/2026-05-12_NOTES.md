## Cleanliness of machine-readable output

- Troy: Terraform recently emitted a deprecation warning to stdout alongside JSON output and broke a bunch of scripts. Do we have an internal rule on this, or is it something we inherited from the fork that we should be mindful of?
- James: Our generalised stance is that machine-readable output (JSON or otherwise) is part of the 1.0 compatibility promise and nothing should be emitted before the data. We won't break that.
- James: Also, OpenTofu's stance is that we will continue supporting the DynamoDB locks for the S3 backend and are not deprecating it, so we wouldn't emit that specific warning anyway.
- Yousif: Common trip-up for Terragrunt users with hooks that print to stdout before commands - parsing JSON-readable output gets harder. I personally really dislike anything altering stdout.
