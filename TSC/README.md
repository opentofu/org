This folder contains the TSC Agendas and Meeting Notes

## Process

Every meeting is run by a member of the TSC.  This responsibility is a rotation between members

### TSC Meeting Responsibilities
* Ensuring the meeting recording is functional.
* Kicking off the meeting once all attendees are present.
* Taking notes or relying on the recording to make notes after.
* Reviewing the action items from the previous TSC meeting.
* Leading the discussion of the topics present in this weeks Agenda.
* Determining who will be the next in the rotation to take on the responsibility.
* Closing the meeting once all topics have been covered or allocated time has elapsed.

### Post TSC Meeting Responsibilities
* Upload the meeting to the OpenTofu YouTube Channel.
* Create a PR to this repo with the following:
  - The `${DATE}_NOTES.md` file with notes from the meeting and link to the video
  - The `${NEXT_DATE}_AGENDA.md` as a stub for anyone to PR items into throughout the two-week period between meetings.

### Recordings

The recordings are stored in the OpenTofu google workspace's drive and can be accessed by all TSC members. They should be uploaded to YouTube under the Creative Commons License (default in our account).
